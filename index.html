Second Majors and Minors

In many cases you can add Electrical Engineering or Systems Science & Engineering as a second major to your primary degree and still complete your degree program in 4 years. Since it is a second major and not a second degree, you will get only one diploma under the name of your primary major and your second major will show in your transcripts. Alternatively, many students who are not ready to commit to a second major, choose to pursue a minor.

The maximum allowed number of either Majors or Minors is three. The overlap of course credit units for any two Minors should not exceed 9 units.
